# PROTOCOL — an example ritual (adapt it to your team)

This is the working ritual the template was born from. It is **an example**, not a
mandate — copy what helps, drop what doesn't.

## Before you touch a project — READ
1. Open `docs/projects/<project>.md`.
2. Read: **Status**, **In progress**, **Next**, **Decisions & risks**.
3. If someone else has an "In progress" item, align before overlapping.

## After you touch it — UPDATE (before you finish)
1. Move what you delivered to **Done** (with a date).
2. Update **In progress** and **Next**.
3. Record new **Decisions & risks**.
4. Bump the header: **Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD by <author>**.
5. Commit.
